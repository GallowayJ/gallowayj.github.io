Interests and hobbies

I my free time I enjoy range of activities from photography, music production and coding to sport and long walks on sunny afternoons. Below you'll be able to find links to my specific web pages relating to these activities.

Photography

I've held a long-standing interest in photograph, recently I've begun the transitionary process of moving from casual to enthusiast. My favourite subjects are landscapes and cityscapes. My entire work-flow is FOSS, I use Darktable as my RAW processor and Gimp running on a Buntu flavour of linux.

Here's a link to my Flickr account.

Coding

Since tinkering with one of my father's games a young boy written in QBasic I've had a keen interest in technology. In 2011 I finally began my journey into programming, starting with the Python programming language. The majority of my use is centred on the statistical and data analysis packages, such as Pandas.

Here is a link to analysis of player movements in the real life game of Turf. To see more of my projects visit my Github page here.

Music and writing

I spend a lot of time listening and dancing to music! In 2009 I decided to try my hand and music production, an experiment which blossomed into a new hobby. Due logistically issues surrounding the transport of my equipment in recent years I've been unable to produce new music. However you can listen to my old tracks here and watch a video from the track Nomad Is My Name here.
